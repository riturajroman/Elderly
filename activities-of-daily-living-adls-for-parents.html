ADLs: What Are Activities of Daily Living ? How Can ADLs Help Your Parents?

All About Assisted Daily Living Care at Home for Elderly Well-Being

We all know that aging is a natural process, and it brings various challenges in everyday activities while performing daily activities. Thus, many elders prefer staying in their homes rather than moving to a nursing facility. To cater for this need, Assisted Daily Living (ADL) care at home is provided as a compassionate solution. It ensures that senior citizens get personalised assistance either from professionals or their family members. This blog will help you understand the significance of home-based assisted living, its services and various benefits.

What Does Assisted Daily Living Care at Home Signify?

Assisted Daily Living (ADL) care is a family-provided or professional support provided to senior citizens in their day-to-day activities. Support is provided to them as they may struggle to perform activities due to illness, disabilities, or ageing. These activities may include dressing, bathing, personal hygiene, medication management, assistance with mobility, and many more. The main goal of providing this care is to help elderly people lead comfortable and independent lives while ensuring their safety.

Various Activities Involved in Daily Living

ADLs can be precisely categorised into two categories based on the tasks involved in them. The two types include basic ADLs and instrumental ADLs. The following are some major activities involved in both of them:

1. Basic ADLs:

Basic ADLs include fundamental tasks necessary for everyday living, such as the following:

  • Personal Hygiene and Bathing
  • Dressing
  • Eating
  • Meal Preparation
  • Mobility (walking, exercising)

All these tasks are essential for all elders and must be taken care of properly.

2. Instrumental ADLs:

Instrumental ADLs involve complex tasks that facilitate independent living, including:

  • Housekeeping
  • Transportation Assistance
  • Financial Management
  • Bill Payments
  • Grocery Shopping

These tasks can be completed by assistants, and they can also help elders in performing these tasks.

It is necessary to help elders while performing these activities. Assistance in these tasks is essential as it can be troublesome for senior citizens and can result in minor or major mishaps. Elderly Wellness provides the best assistance in this regard and helps elders to perform their tasks seamlessly.

Major Benefits of Assisted Daily Living Care at Home

There are several benefits of having assisted daily living care at home. These benefits will help you understand the need for assisted daily living care for your loved ones. Take a look at some of the most important benefits of this process:

1. Personal Independence:

Senior citizens face a major concern of losing their independence while aging, making them feel dependent on others. As a result, to resolve this issue, home-based assisted living ensures that they receive the best possible support, along with maintaining control over their daily lifestyle.

2. Comfortable and Familiar Environment:

A familiar and comfortable atmosphere for your loved ones is a must, as it has positive effects on mental and emotional well-being. Also, senior citizens feel more comfortable and safer in their homes. It helps them to reduce their stress and anxiety, thus improving their daily lifestyle significantly.

3. Personalized Care:

In-home care services provide tailored plans as per a person’s daily needs. It is very different from nursing homes and assisted living facilities, and it provides a healthier environment for the elders. The personalised care can be either provided full-time or part-time as per the individual’s needs.

4. Cost-Effective Alternative:

Nursing homes can turn out expensive as they have several costs associated with them. On the other hand, home care services are cost-effective, along with providing quality care. Also, families can customise the type of support based on their needs, making it more financially viable and easier.

5. Fall Prevention and Safety:

Senior citizens can get injured severely if they fall from a place. As a result, to prevent such accidents, assisted daily living caregivers provide proper assistance to them, ensuring their safety. Hence, it reduces the risk of accidents or falls and also helps them to live independently in a safer environment.

All these benefits significantly improve the lives of elders and help them to lead a good life post-retirement. As a result, home care services are very essential to provide the best atmosphere to your loved ones.

Essential Services Provided in Assisted Daily Living Care at Home

There are several services involved in assisted daily living care provided to elders. Some of these most important services are as follows:

1. Personal Care:

Elders need assistance with personal care on a day-to-day basis. It includes help and support while performing activities like bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting assistance. Also, major help is needed for mobility (walking and exercising), eating, and drinking to make their lives easier.

2. Proper Medication:

Senior citizens are supposed to take their medicines on time, and proper assistance is essential for this purpose. Hence, caregivers assist them by reminding them to take the prescribed medications on time. Also, they monitor their potential side effects and ensure proper dosage at the right time.

3. Meal Preparation:

Support regarding proper nutrition and meal preparation is another task necessary for the elderly well-being. Hence, preparing well-balanced meals and feeding them, if needed, can be provided by caregivers. Also, encouraging a healthy diet based on their medical conditions is essential.

4. Support with Mobility:

Caregivers and family members provide support to seniors while moving around the house. Also, assistance with transportation for doctor’s appointments, social outings, and grocery shopping is much needed. In all these aspects, home-care services help senior citizens in the best possible way.

5. Companionship and Emotional Support:

Lastly, home-care services provide emotional support and companionship to your loved ones. They engage in meaningful conversations, encourage social activities, and provide emotional support. It helps elders to lead a stress and anxiety-free life, thus ensuring happiness in their everyday lives.

These were some of the essential services provided in assisted daily living care for elders. However, as per other different needs, caregivers provide support in that aspect too.
